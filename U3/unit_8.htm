Unit 8

A - Understand the characteristics and uses of mobile apps

I was asked to review two different kind of mobile app and to prepare a short report which include: an explanation of the intended use and features of them; a review of features they have and discussion of the strengths and weaknesses of them.

B - Design a mobile app

I was asked to create a design for interactive app, that will help children between 3 & 7 years old to learn the alphabet. User interface should be appealing to this age range and simple to use. The app need to help the users recognise the shapes and sounds of letters of the alphabet.

C - Develop and test a mobile app

The development process include creating a multi-screen interface which demonstrates an awareness of the user requirements and intended purpose of the app. The app is multifunctional. I edited defended codes and developed some original codes to fulfill the design requirements of the app. I tested the app for functionality and purpose and repaired all the found faults.

D - Review the finished mobile app

I reviewed why the app is suitable for the user requirements; for the target audience and for the intended purpose. I explained how the testing, the user feedback and the constraints have affected the suitability of the app. I justified all the changes I have made, while I evaluated my initial design and the completed app.

Unit grade - Pass

I learned how mobile apps are developed. I learned how to design and set blocks with codes to make my app works. I used predefined codes, I change some of them to make them suitable for my design and even builded my own. I enjoyed this unit, while learning how to create apps that I thought I only can use.
